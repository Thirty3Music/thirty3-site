PROJECT

1159 ● Music & Sound Design

“1159” is a short film exploring loneliness in the metropolis.

Directed and created by Petter Scholander. For this project I composed the original music and crafted the sound design, supporting his vision and atmosphere. A large part of the work was field recording – capturing sounds from my hometown and from London – weaving them together into an immersive soundscape that carries the feeling of urban isolation.
